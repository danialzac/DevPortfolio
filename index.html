Hi, I'm Danial Zac, a Full Stack Developer with experience across JavaScript, React, Java, and Spring Boot, backed by a strong foundation in software testing within banking, insurance, and public sector systems.

My background has given me deep exposure to API-driven architecture, backend integration, data validation, and compliance-focused environments where precision matters. Over the years, I've developed a way of working that is both strategic and hands-on. I learn quickly, adapt with purpose, and stay composed under pressure.

I'm comfortable taking ownership, aligning stakeholders, improving processes, and helping teams move forward with structure and clarity. That combination of technical execution, analytical thinking, and leadership responsibility has shaped how I build software today.

Outside of software, I value disciplines that sharpen focus, patience, and consistency. Martial arts is one of the practices that has influenced how I approach growth, execution, and composure under pressure.

I care about creating stable, scalable, and user-centered applications that are not only functional, but dependable. I'm looking to contribute to a strong team where I can keep learning, take on meaningful responsibility, and help turn complex problems into clean, well-executed solutions.
