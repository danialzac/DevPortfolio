Hi, I'm Danial Zac, a developer with hands-on experience across JavaScript, React, Java, and Spring Boot, backed by a strong foundation in software testing within banking, insurance, public sector, and real estate-related systems.

I learn best by building completed projects end to end. That process helps me strengthen backend and frontend execution, improve product thinking, and turn new tools into software I can explain, maintain, and keep improving.

I'm looking to contribute to a strong team where I can keep learning, take ownership, and help turn challenging problems into dependable, well-executed software.
